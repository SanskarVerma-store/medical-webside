About Us

A trusted name in neighbourhood healthcare

Verma Medical Store is a community pharmacy dedicated to reliable medical supplies, wellness products, and essential healthcare solutions — built on a legacy of service since 2001.

Verma Medical bridges the gap between traditional care and modern health management. By offering an extensive range of prescription medications, over-the-counter drugs, and holistic wellness products, we ensure comprehensive health support is always within reach.

Led by a Licensed Pharmacist and supported by two dedicated Pharmacy Workers, our three-person team combines 25 years of experience into a streamlined, patient-first operation — from clinical accuracy at the counter to fast billing and organised queue management.
